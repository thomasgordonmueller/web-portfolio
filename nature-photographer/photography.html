What make's John Smiths photography unique?

Editing Style

I color correct every photo individually so each photo looks its best. All of my photos are bright, colorful, with color accurate tones, and sharp. A lot of photographers use a preset filter and apply it to all their photos that will ultimately look dated in a few years. You will not see any cheesy instagram-like filters on your professional photos from me.

Custom Shots

I am one of the only nature photographers that delivers custom nature shots at an affordable price point. I will travel to various regions on the globe and search for the perfect conditions for all of my clients. This is a unique part of my buisness model which helps me deliver wonderful products while putting the customers needs first.

Image Release 10+ Year Gallery

All my clients receive their high resolution photos with printing release at no extra charge. I want you to be able to print and share your photos as you wish. Your wedding photos are delivered via download from an online gallery. This gallery is available for 10 years in case you misplace your downloaded photos. Plus you can share this gallery with anyone so you wont have to make everyone a copy.

Scheduling Master

I have shot hundreds of weddings so I have perfected making wedding day photo schedules. I want you to get all the beautiful shots you want and still get to your reception to party on time. We will work together to craft the perfect schedule for your wedding day photography.

Fast Turn Around

A lot of photographers take 5 months to even a YEAR to deliver their clients wedding photos! That is way too long. I will have your edited wedding photos ready for you within 2 months. If you decided to bundle video with your photos then your video will be ready within 3 months.

Add Video Coverage

I started off as a filmmaker and have won over 30 local and national video awards. So I have a big passion for shooting and editing captivating stories. For this reason I have multiple wedding video packages including a highlights video only option. Wedding videos are shot by a video team who were taught my style so your photography and videography style match.
